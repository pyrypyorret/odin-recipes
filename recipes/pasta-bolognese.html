Pasta Bolognese

Delicious bolognese

This recipe makes a true classic

Ingredients

  • 170g bacon
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 gloves of garlic
  • 150g carrot
  • 100g celery
  • 400g minced beef meat
  • 500g crushed tomatoes
  • 2dl red wine
  • 1dl water
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp oregano
  • 1/4tsp black pepper
  • 1/4tsp ground nutmeg
  • 400g spaghetti

In addition

  • parmiggiano reggiano

Steps

  1. Cut the bacon into thin strips. Finely chop the onion and garlic cloves.
  2. Cut carrots and celery into very small cubes.
  3. Spread the shredded bacon in a large frying pan and heat slowly to release the liquid and fat. Raise the heat and fry until the bacon is browned.
  4. Add minced meat and brown. Also add onion, garlic and vegetables. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Pour crushed tomatoes, wine and water into the saucepan. Season with salt, oregano, pepper and nutmeg.
  6. Simmer over low heat under the lid for about 45 minutes. Stir occasionally and add a little water if necessary.
  7. Cook the pasta in plenty of salted water according to the instructions on the package. Drain the pasta.
  8. Dispense the pasta on plates. Top with sauce and sprinkle with grated Parmesan or shavings.
