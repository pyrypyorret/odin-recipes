Pasta Alfredo

Delicious italian Pasta alfredo

This recipe makes authentic Italian pasta Alfredo

Ingredients

2-3 portions

  • 250g tagliatelle
  • 25g butter
  • 1-2 gloves of garlic
  • 2dl cream
  • 1dl grated parmiggiano reggiano
  • 1/4tsp black pepper
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • dash of pasta water

In addition

  • 1/2dl fresh parsley
  • 1/2dl grated parmiggiano reggiano

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water (2 teaspoons salt/2 litres of water) according to the instructions on the package until it is almost cooked.
  2. Peel and slice the garlic cloves. In a pan, melt the butter and add the garlic. Listen for a moment. Add the cream, grated Parmesan, black pepper and salt. Heat the sauce.
  3. Drain the pasta and collect some cooking water. Add the pasta to the sauce. Cook the pasta to the end in a pan. Add the appropriate amount of cooking water.
  4. Add parsley and parmesan. Serve the pasta immediately.
