Lasagna

Juicy homemade lasagna

This recipe makes a delicious home kitchen classic

Ingredients

Minced meat sauce

  • 9 pasta sheets
  • 3 onions
  • 4-5 garlic gloves
  • 1tbsp Valio Oivariini
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 1dl water
  • 2 meat broth cubes
  • 1tbsp sugar
  • 1/4tsp white pepper

Cheese sauce

  • 3tbsp Valio Oivariini
  • 3tbsp all purpose flour
  • 6dl Valio Kiehu
  • 150g grated Emmental cheese
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/4tsp white pepper

For glazing

  • 1-2tbsp Valio oivariini

On top

  • 1dl grated cheese

Steps

  1. On the bottom of the greased lasagna tin, first spread the minced meat sauce and then the cheese sauce.
    Put 3 lasagna plates on top. Put minced meat sauce and cheese sauce on top again. Prepare the following layers in the same way.
  2. Finally, press the lasagna lightly so that the sauces are evenly distributed. Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
  3. Cook at 175 degrees for approx. 1 hour. Let stand for 15-20 minutes before serving.
